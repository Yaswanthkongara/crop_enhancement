Welcome to the Agricultural Recommendation System

This system helps you choose the right crops and fertilizers based on soil conditions, weather, and more.

Rice: Botanical Name: *Oryza sativa*
Growing Conditions: Needs warm, tropical/subtropical climates with flooded fields.
Nutritional Profile: High in carbs, moderate protein, rich in B vitamins (especially in brown rice).

Maize: Botanical Name: *Zea mays*
Growing Conditions: Moderate temperatures and full sunlight; requires good drainage.
Nutritional Profile: High in carbohydrates, contains some protein, rich in vitamin B6 and antioxidants.

Jute: Botanical Name: *Corchorus olitorius*
Growing Conditions: Warm and humid climates, thrives in river valleys.
Uses: Primarily used for making textiles and sacks; environmentally friendly fiber.

Groundnut: Botanical Name: *Arachis hypogaea*
Growing Conditions: Prefers warm, sunny environments with well-drained soil.
Nutritional Profile: High in protein, healthy fats, and vitamin E.

Cotton: Botanical Name: *Gossypium spp.*
Growing Conditions: Requires warm, sunny climates with moderate rainfall.
Uses: Fiber for textiles; cottonseed oil and animal feed also derived from seeds.

Coconut: Botanical Name: *Cocos nucifera*
Growing Conditions: Grows well in tropical, coastal regions with sandy soils.
Uses: Fruit, oil, milk, and coir for a wide range of culinary and industrial products.

Papaya: Botanical Name: *Carica papaya*
Growing Conditions: Requires warm, frost-free climates with well-drained soil.
Nutritional Profile: High in vitamin C and papain, aiding digestion.

Orange: Botanical Name: *Citrus sinensis*
Growing Conditions: Thrives in subtropical climates with well-drained soils.
Nutritional Profile: High in vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants.

Apple: Botanical Name: *Malus domestica*
Growing Conditions: Prefers temperate climates with cool winters.
Nutritional Profile: Rich in fiber, vitamin C, and various antioxidants.

Muskmelon: Botanical Name: *Cucumis melo*
Growing Conditions: Requires warm temperatures and well-drained soil.
Nutritional Profile: High in vitamins A and C, hydrating with natural sugars.

Watermelon: Botanical Name: *Citrullus lanatus*
Growing Conditions: Warm climate, well-drained sandy or loamy soil.
Nutritional Profile: Hydrating, high in vitamins A and C, and antioxidants like lycopene.

Grapes: Botanical Name: *Vitis vinifera*
Growing Conditions: Grows in warm climates with well-drained soil.
Nutritional Profile: High in antioxidants, including resveratrol, and vitamin K.

Mango: Botanical Name: *Mangifera indica*
Growing Conditions: Requires tropical climates and well-drained soil.
Nutritional Profile: High in vitamins A, C, and fiber.

Banana: Botanical Name: *Musa spp.*
Growing Conditions: Warm, tropical climates with ample water.
Nutritional Profile: Rich in potassium, fiber, and natural sugars.

Pomegranate: Botanical Name: *Punica granatum*
Growing Conditions: Grows in semi-arid to tropical climates.
Nutritional Profile: High in vitamin C, antioxidants, and polyphenols.
